This video was made for my final assignment for my ART-75 class. I wanted to make a strong message for the art piece with myself , my other classmates, as well as the other students living at San Jose State University campus. This video inscribes the narrative of some students and how they are feeling this year during the COVID pandemic occurring. I made this video so people will be able to see it and understand the learning process that students are going through having it be during a difficult time.
